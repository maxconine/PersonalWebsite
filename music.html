My Music Journey

I play mainly electric guitar and write music as well as do live performances. In high school I played bass and guitar for my school’s Jazz Ensemble and also wrote songs with my brother for our band QandM. In college I am part of a few bands, mamak and 6th Street Boogie. I am also the co‑president of the Harvey Mudd Jam Society so I organize events and take care of the equipment in the room. I also manage the club’s budget and equipment purchasing.

QandM

QandM is a band I started with my brother. We started recording music in 2017 and have been producing music since. New Album being released soon!

We posted our first albums to bandcamp: qandm.bandcamp.com

You can also find our music on Apple Music and Spotify!
